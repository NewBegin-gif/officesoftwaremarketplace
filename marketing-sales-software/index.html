Frequently asked questions

What software do I actually need to grow revenue?

Most small teams need three things: a way to reach people (email or ads), a way to track deals (a CRM), and a way to see what's working (analytics). Start with the one bottleneck costing you most right now instead of buying a whole suite at once.

Is there free marketing and sales software?

Yes — many tools here offer a genuine free tier or trial, especially email and CRM tools. Start free, prove the workflow, then upgrade only when a limit actually blocks you. Free plans change often, so check each provider's current limits.
