Frequently asked questions

What financial software does a small business need?

Usually three layers: a way to get paid (payments and invoicing), a way to pay people (payroll), and a way to track spend (expense management). Start with whichever is eating the most of your time or causing the most errors.

Is there free accounting or expense software?

Some tools offer free tiers or trials, particularly for expense tracking and invoicing. For payroll and tax, free options are rarer because compliance is involved. Verify current pricing and regional support before committing.

How do I choose a finance tool I can trust?
