Frequently asked questions

What's the best business phone setup for a small team?

It depends on volume: app-first virtual numbers suit founders and small teams, while smart call-routing and contact-centre features matter once you have a dedicated support or sales desk. Don't pay for contact-centre features you won't use.

Can AI voice agents replace a call centre?

For routine, repetitive calls they can deflect a lot of volume — but they work best alongside humans, not instead of them. Start by automating the most repetitive enquiries and always keep an easy path to a person.

Is there free communication software?

Many tools offer free trials or starter tiers. Test call quality and reliability with your real team before rolling out — that's exactly what free trials are for. Check current limits on the provider's site.
